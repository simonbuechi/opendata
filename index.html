What I am doing these days

Raisin Picker

Raisin Picker is a personal knowledge management tool. It features tagging, rating and linking of content nodes. Using Apache Solr, a faceted search enables you to find content quickly. It is based on Drupal content management system and is available as open source.

Fractile

Fractile is my startup project.
Problem: If you work on a computer, you might waste your a lot of your time with trivial tasks (email, chat) and procrastination (Facebook, news sites). In brief, you are not as productive as you could be.
Solution: Fractile evaluates your behaviour and prompts alerts, when you are distracted and provides tips, how to work more productive.
Status: Prototyping, company not yet founded.
